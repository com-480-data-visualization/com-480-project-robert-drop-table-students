SPEAKERS

How many different speakers have visited TED Talks? How many speeches has each had? Who's been the most popular speaker by count of talks or by views?

With this graph you can examine speaker data and find interesting information about TED speakers beginning from year 1972. Each bubble represents one speaker. The size of the bubble tells how many viewing times has each speaker has had in their speeches by total. The color of the bubble, on the other hand, tells the number of talks per speaker.

    Controls:
  1. Group bubbles by clicking the buttons above the graph.
  2. With range panel, you can choose the number of the bubbles on the graph.
    • Range drops out speakers starting from smallest number of talks with least viewing times.
  3. Hover over a bubble to see information about the speaker.
